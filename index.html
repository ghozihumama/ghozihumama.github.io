About Us

Awesome,Clean,
Poweful and Creative!

We’re here to help finding nearby venue, reserve table, order menu and payment process easier with more than 500 Nightlife Outlet recommendations in Indonesia. Our Vision is to meet users and nightlife enthusiasts with their best interest


An application-based company which helps and benefits by connecting users with joining outlet partners. We guarantee Ravve users to enjoy the experience in deciding reservation with “nearby” feature and facilitate outlets to introduce their Club, Bar, Pub and Lounge within apps feature. Users will get points for every bookings and payments through Ravve in exchange for exclusive gifts and delightful offers. Outlets can develop and introduce their outlets and ongoing events to users with in-app promotions, notification blast feature in Ravve apps.
